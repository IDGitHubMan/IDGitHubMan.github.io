Processing

Processing is a highly visual language based on Java. It is often used for interactive displays and graphics. I only recently picked it up, but have made a few different projects most of which are attempts at emulating something I saw that snagged my interest.

I have developed five or seven things in processing, and it is one of the languages I hope to master due to it's versatility (usable across Android, Windows, Mac, Online), simplicity (basic and easy to learn syntax), and general usage as an interactive program development language. I hope to advance to the point of making advanced and interactive simulations, including things like cellular automata. Unfortunately, I'm not at that point yet. Most of these projects were created in processing then ported over to P5JS with minimal changes.
